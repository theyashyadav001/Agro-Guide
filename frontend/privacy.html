🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

Data is collected only after user permission and consent. AgroGuide is designed with your privacy as a top priority. We are fully transparent about what data is collected, when, and why.

What We Collect

We do not collect any data automatically or in the background. Data is only shared when you:

  • Tap the "Share My Data" button
  • Review and accept the permission rationale
  • Explicitly agree in the consent dialog

Data You May Choose to Share

  • 📍 Location — Your current GPS coordinates, sent as a Google Maps link
  • 🖼️ Image — A single image you manually pick from your gallery
  • 👤 Contact — A name and phone number you type in yourself

How Data Is Used

Data you choose to share is sent directly to a configured Telegram channel for support and assistance purposes. It is used solely for the purpose you agree to at the time of sharing.

Data Storage

AgroGuide does not store any personal data on its own servers. No data is retained after it is sent.

Third-Party Services

  • Telegram Bot API — Used to deliver data you choose to share
  • OpenWeatherMap / Weather API — Used to fetch weather data for crop recommendations
  • Railway / Render — Backend hosting for crop recommendation AI

Your Rights

  • You can cancel the sharing flow at any point before sending
  • No data is ever collected without your explicit action and consent
  • You can deny location access at any time via your browser or device settings

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the app developer via the AgroGuide Telegram bot.
